Since we were founded in 1984

A Brief History of the GymOn Fitness Center

1984 - 1990

The Early Days of GymOn

Founded in 1984, GymOn started as a small fitness studio offering basic training programs. It quickly grew in popularity due to its focus on personalized training and modern equipment.

1991 - 2000

Expansion and Recognition

By the mid-90s, GymOn expanded to multiple locations, earning recognition for its innovative approach to fitness. It introduced a wide range of fitness classes and expert trainers.

2001 - 2010

The Rise of Functional Training

During this decade, GymOn became a leader in functional training, offering high-intensity classes and introducing state-of-the-art equipment to its gyms. The membership base grew significantly.

2011 - Present

A Global Fitness Leader

GymOn continued to expand globally, now offering online training programs and partnerships with fitness influencers. It remains committed to providing cutting-edge fitness solutions.
